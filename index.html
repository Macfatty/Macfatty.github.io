About

I am a DevOps Engineer student building real systems alongside my full-time work as a forklift driver in a warehouse, where I also take on administrative responsibilities. Annos is my own project and the main place where I bring together fullstack development, infrastructure, security and operational thinking. During my internship at Digital Democracy, an ideal-driven organization, I worked in a DevOps role where I helped develop and implement the infrastructure their application runs on. After the internship ended, I stayed on as a volunteer because the experience continues to be valuable to me, and because I enjoy contributing to open-source work outside my regular job.

My main project is Annos, a fullstack food ordering platform with role-based access, Dockerized services, PostgreSQL, CI/CD, security hardening and production deployment preparation.

Annos Platform

Annos is a fullstack food ordering platform designed around four user roles: customer, restaurant, courier and admin.

  • JWT authentication
  • Role-based access control
  • Rate limiting on authentication routes
  • GDPR export and deletion endpoints
  • Non-root Docker containers
